# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors and users of KushCloud. We do not tolerate harassment or discrimination of any kind.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

* Using welcoming and inclusive language
* Being respectful of differing opinions, experiences, and backgrounds
* Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
* Focusing on what is best for the community
* Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment, insulting/derogatory comments
* Discrimination based on protected characteristics
* Public or private attacks on individuals
* Publishing private information without consent
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior. They will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to instances of unacceptable behavior.

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers at [@Linacre](https://github.com/LIN4CRE) or by opening a confidential issue.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces associated with KushCloud, including GitHub issues, discussions, and pull requests.
