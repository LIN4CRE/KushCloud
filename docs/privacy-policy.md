# Privacy Policy — KushCloud

**Last updated:** July 12, 2026

## 1. Introduction

KushCloud ("we," "our," or "us") is an open-source web game available at [https://lin4cre.github.io/KushCloud/](https://lin4cre.github.io/KushCloud/). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play KushCloud.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you play KushCloud, the following information is stored **locally on your device** using the browser's localStorage:

- A randomly generated player ID
- A randomly generated display name
- Your game statistics (scores, coins, games played, etc.)
- Your unlocked items (skins, trails, power-ups)
- Your settings preferences (sound volume, accessibility options)
- Your daily reward streak

This data **never leaves your device** unless you choose to interact with the online leaderboard.

### 2.2 Online Leaderboard Data

If you access the online leaderboard feature, the following information is transmitted to and stored on **Firebase Realtime Database** (operated by Google LLC):

- Your randomly generated display name
- Your game scores
- Timestamps of your runs

**No personally identifiable information** (such as your real name, email address, or IP address) is intentionally collected or stored by KushCloud.

### 2.3 Third-Party Services

KushCloud uses **Firebase** (Google LLC) for the online leaderboard. Firebase may collect technical information such as IP addresses and usage data in accordance with [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- **Local data** (localStorage): To save your game progress, settings, and preferences between sessions.
- **Leaderboard data** (Firebase): To display your scores on the public leaderboard alongside your display name.

We do **not**:

- Sell your personal information
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Track you across other websites
- Collect your real name, email, or contact information

## 4. Your Choices and Controls

- **Clear local data:** You can clear all local game data by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site. This will reset all progress.
- **Change display name:** You can change your randomly generated display name in the game Settings.
- **Opt out of leaderboard:** If you do not wish to participate in the online leaderboard, avoid accessing the Scores tab. Your scores will remain stored locally only.

## 5. Children's Privacy

KushCloud is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

- Communication with Firebase is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
- Local data is stored only on your device
- Firebase security rules limit database access

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the game and in the GitHub repository.
